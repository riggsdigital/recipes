Sheet Pan Chicken with Vegetables

A simple one-pan dinner — chicken thighs roasted with potatoes and vegetables.

−
4 servings
+

Ingredients

  • 8 boneless skinless chicken thighs
  • 1.5 pounds baby potatoes, halved
  • 1 pound broccoli florets
  • 2 red bell peppers, sliced
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 0.5 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 lemon, cut into wedges

Steps

  1. 1
    Preheat oven

    Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C). Line a large sheet pan with foil or parchment.

  2. 2
    Toss potatoes

    Toss baby potatoes with 1 tbsp of the olive oil, half the garlic, and a pinch of salt and pepper. Spread on the pan and roast alone to give them a head start.

    ⏰ 10:00
  3. 3
    Season chicken and vegetables

    While potatoes roast, toss chicken thighs, broccoli, and bell peppers with remaining olive oil, garlic, smoked paprika, oregano, salt, and pepper.

  4. 4
    Roast everything together

    Add the chicken and vegetables to the pan with the potatoes, spreading everything in a single layer. Roast about 20 minutes, until chicken is cooked through (internal temp 165°F) and vegetables are tender.

  5. 5
    Rest and serve

    Let chicken rest, then squeeze lemon wedges over everything before serving.

    ⏰ 05:00

Notes

Swap in green beans, zucchini, or carrots for the broccoli/pepper if you prefer. Potatoes are fine here — just keep portions in mind if you're watching total carbs for the day.
